EMERGENCY FIRST AID

Emergency First Aid focuses on recognising and treating life threatening conditions. In addition, it equips the participant with the knowledge, skillset and confidence to effectively deal with common injures and ailments.

Course Objectives

  • Recognising and treating life threatening conditions
  • Competently administering first aid treatment
  • Correctly using an automated external defibrillator (AED)

Course Content

  • First-Aid in the Workplace
  • Patient Assessment
  • Respiratory Emergencies
  • Cardiac First Response
  • Wounds and Bleeding
  • Altered Levels of Consciousness
  • Musculoskeletal Injuries
  • Burns and Scalds, Chemicals and Electric Shock
