About Us

Providing high quality private and public first aid training courses.

Established in 2014, First Aid Responder Training Ltd has grown significantly in the delivery of high quality private and public first aid training courses. Based in the South East of Ireland we have delivered courses across Ireland over the years to the companies, state organisations and community groups. Year after year our growth has continued due to word or mouth and recurring business. All our staff are Garda Vetted and are compelled to engage in continuous professional development to maintain their skills to ensure we deliver a high quality service.

First Aid Responder Training Ltd provides courses accredited to recongised organisations so you can be assured you are getting quality courses delivered to you.

  • QQI - FETAC - Quality & Qualifications Ireland
  • PHECC - Pre-Hospital Emergency Care Council
  • IHF - Irish Heart Foundation
  • OFAAA - Occupational First Aid Assessment Agent Centre
